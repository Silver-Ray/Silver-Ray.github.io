MATLAB-01基本操作和矩阵输入

发表于2023-10-09|更新于2023-09-28
|阅读量:

不声明时默认的类型时double

Special Variables and Constants

  • ans

  • i, j : complex number

  • Inf : ∞

  • eps : epsilon, 2.2204e-16

  • NaN : not a number

  • pi : π

  • To list Keywords:

    • >> iskeyword

Array(Vector and Matrix)

Output:

$$
a*b=30
$$

$$
b*a =\left(\begin{array}{cccc}
1 & 2 & 3 & 4\
2 & 4 & 6 & 8\
3 & 6 & 9 & 12\
4 & 8 & 12 & 16
\end{array}\right)
$$

用空格(space)隔开行元素，用分号(semicolon)隔开不同的列元素

Array Indexing

  1. A(n): 取出矩阵的第n个元素（MATLAB采用列优先）
  2. A(row, column): 取出矩阵第row行第column列的元素
  3. A(Matrix or Vector): 使用矩阵或者向量的值索引，返回一个按原方式排列的矩阵或向量
  4. A(row vector, column vector): 使用第一个向量作为行数，第二个向量作为列数，取二者交叉元素

Colon Operator

create a array: start: step: stop, different from pyton: range(start, stop, step)

使用冒号运算符清空矩阵中的某一行或者某一列：

A(3,:) = []: 清空第三行，这里的[ ]表示empty

Array Concatenation

Output:

$$
A = \left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2\ 3 & 4 \end{array}\right)\
B = \left(\begin{array}{cc} 9 & 9\ 9 & 9 \end{array}\right)\
C = \left(\begin{array}{cccc} 1 & 2 & 9 & 9\ 3 & 4 & 9 & 9 \end{array}\right)\
D = \left(\begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2\ 3 & 4\ 9 & 9\ 9 & 9 \end{array}\right)
$$

Array Manipulation

Operators on array: + - * / ^ . '

注意：当a为数值时，A.*a和A*a相同，A.\a和A\a相同，但是A^a和A.^a不同

Some Special Matrix

  • linspace(): linearly spaced vectors

    • linspace(start, stop, number)

  • eys(): n*n identity matrix

  • zeros(n1, n2): n1*n2 zero matrix

  • ones(n1, n2): n1*n2 matrix with every entry as 1

  • diag(): diagonal matrix

  • rand(): uniformly distributed random numbers

Some Matrix Function

column width function

For example:

$$
\begin{aligned}
M = \left(\begin{array}{cccc} 16 & 2 & 3 & 13\ 5 & 11 & 10 & 8\ 9 & 7 & 6 & 12\ 4 & 14 & 15 & 1 \end{array}\right)\\

\max(M) = \left(\begin{array}{cccc}16 & 14 & 15 & 13\end{array} \right)\\

\max(\max(M)) = 16\\

\mathrm{sum}(M)=\left(\begin{array}{cccc} 34 & 34 & 34 & 34 \end{array}\right)\\

\mathrm{sum}(\mathrm{sum}) = 136
\end{aligned}

$$

The difference between sort and sortrows:

sort函数会将每一个row的内部进行排序后输出，而sortrows函数则只会比较第一个row，它将每一个column视为整体进行排序：

For example:

$$
\begin{aligned}
M = \left(\begin{array}{cccc} 16 & 2 & 3 & 13\ 5 & 11 & 10 & 8\ 9 & 7 & 6 & 12\ 4 & 14 & 15 & 1 \end{array}\right)\\

\mathrm{sort}(M) = \left(\begin{array}{cccc} 4 & 2 & 3 & 1\ 5 & 7 & 6 & 8\ 9 & 11 & 10 & 12\ 16 & 14 & 15 & 13 \end{array}\right)\\

\mathrm{sortrows}(M) = \left(\begin{array}{cccc} 4 & 14 & 15 & 1\ 5 & 11 & 10 & 8\ 9 & 7 & 6 & 12\ 16 & 2 & 3 & 13 \end{array}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$
